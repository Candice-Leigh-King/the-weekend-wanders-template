WELCOME! We're Tara and Brittany and our journey began with a shared passion for travel and a desire to create a space where LGBTQ individuals could find inspiration, guidance, and a sense of belonging in their wanderlust pursuits. Drawing from our own experiences as queer travelers, we embarked on a mission to uncover hidden gems, celebrate diverse cultures, and showcase LGBTQ-friendly destinations around the globe.

What sets us apart is not just our love for adventure, but our unwavering dedication to amplifying LGBTQ voices in the travel industry. We recognize the importance of representation, as LGBTQ travelers still face challenges and barriers. We strive to highlight the experiences of LGBTQ travelers, from navigating unfamiliar terrains to finding welcoming spaces in every corner of the world. From destination guides, travel tips, to collaborating with LGBTQ-owned businesses, our platform serves as a beacon of empowerment for queer travelers everywhere.

Weekends are sacred to us, reserved for embarking on spontaneous road trips, exploring nearby cities, or immersing ourselves in the natural wonders of the great outdoors. Whether it's hiking through scenic trails, camping under the stars, or indulging in local cuisine, we approach each adventure with joy. 

We have a dog, Remy, who comes on our adventures when possible. We  are committed to our mission of making the world a more inclusive and welcoming place for LGBTQ travelers. With each adventure, we redefine what it means to travel authentically, fearlessly, and unapologetically as members of the queer community.

We're so glad you're here and can't wait to connect more with you!

-Tara and Brittany
